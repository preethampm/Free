# Feedback

A feedback platform for any event. Create your system, generate QR codes, let people rate, and see results live.

## How It Works

**For organizers:**
1. Sign in with Google
2. Create an event (e.g., "College Fest 2026")
3. Add what people will rate (games, stalls, rooms, etc.)
4. Set what they rate on (fun factor, presentation, etc.)
5. Download QR codes — one for each item
6. Print and place QR codes at each item
7. Watch results come in live

**For attendees:**
1. Scan the QR code at an item
2. Enter your name and phone number
3. Rate each item on the given criteria
4. Leave optional feedback
5. Rate all items for your votes to count

## What You Get

- QR codes for each item — print and stick at the booth
- Real-time leaderboard — see scores update as people vote
- Per-criterion breakdown — not just one average score
- Progress tracking — attendees see how many items they've rated
- CSV export — download results anytime
